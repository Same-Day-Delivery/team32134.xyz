About Us

We’re Team 32134, Same Day Delivery, one of the only FTC robotics teams in Western Sydney. We are five high school students passionate about engineering, and our mission is to bring STEM opportunities to over 300 local students through both robotics and other programs, many from low-SES backgrounds.

What is FTC?

The FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is an international robotics competition where students design, build, and program robots to compete at regional, national, and even international levels.
